Payment Basics


  • Fees are due each Friday for the week we have just completed, unless you have already established a different payment plan with the director.
  • You will receive a one week grace period after the due date to make up your payment. If after that time you have not made a payment, your children will not be able to return. They may return as soon as a payment is made if the space is still available.
  • Fees are due regardless of attendance. Each family will receive two weeks of vacation during the calendar year in which you will not be required to pay your fee. Please let us know of your vacation in advance so we may appropriately schedule our staff members.
  • If a check is bounced, you will be charged $20.00. If a second check is bounched, you will pay an additional $20.00 and, from that point forward, will only be allowed to pay in cash.
