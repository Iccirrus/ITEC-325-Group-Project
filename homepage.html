Welcome to the R.O.C.K. Club!

"With the Lord's help, we want to become one of the friendliest places a child can come to after school each day in Radford. We want to be the kind of place that you are proud to have your child attend."

Here at the R.O.C.K. Club, we offer an exciting way of filling your child's hours - at the center as well as outside of school - centered in a loving Christian environment. Our pre-school program encourages your child's development by offering Kindergarten-readiness activities, and our school-age program supports your child's progress by offering tutoring and homework assistance. Every child at the R.O.C.K. Club is given assistance in their spiritual development as we share basic Biblical principles.


Our vision is to serve the community of Radford by providing exceptional childcare for all children ages 3—12. We accomplish this through a balanced program designed to meet the needs of the whole child. Our ministry will help equip children with the tools to succeed, and we will walk with them as they develop spiritually, intellectually, physically, socially, and emotionally. We believe in Proverbs 22:6, which says:

"Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it."
Proverbs 22:6, ESV


Our facility is located at 1820 2nd Street in the west end of Radford. The Radford Worship Center has freely given our program access to the fellowship hall, gymnasium, playground, and youth sanctuary for our activities.


Thank you for considering the R.O.C.K. Club as your childcare provider! If you have any questions, please contact us via the contact link above!
