Small groups - Kids will split up into small groups for an age appropriate Bible lesson that corresponds to the theme. The groups will rotate between arts & crafts, group building games, and other activities that reinforce the theme for the week.

Cleanup and preparation for lunch.

Lunch is served - Group A will eat lunch first.

Group A leaves for the pool, Group B goes to the upstairs game room.
3:30PM
Group A returns from the pool, cleanup and preparation for snack time.
4:00PM
Snack time.
4:30PM
Afternoon large group - We will gather for music, games, and a review of the day’s lesson.

Go home.
