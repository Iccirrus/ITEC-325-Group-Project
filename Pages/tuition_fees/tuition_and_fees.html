School-Age Tuition

After-School Weekly Fees

For grades K-6

If your annual household income is $15,000 or under...
You pay $15.00 per child, per week.
If your annual household income is between $15,001 and $30,000...
You pay $25.00 per child, per week.
If your annual household income is $30,001 or higher...
You pay $45.00 per child, per week.

Part-Time Weekly Fees

If your child will only attend two days per week or fewer...
Your fee will be half of the standard weekly after-school fee for your income bracket.

Before-School Weekly Fees

If you do not need before-school care...
No additional charges will apply.
If you need before-school and after-school care...
Add $15.00 per child, per week to your standard weekly after-school fee.
If you need before-school care only...
You pay $25.00 per child, per week.

Pre-School Tuition

Pre-School Weekly Fees

If your annual household income is $15,000 or under...
You pay $50.00 per child, per week.
If your annual household income is between $15,001 and $30,000...
You pay $70.00 per child, per week.
If your annual household income is $30,001 or higher...
You pay $90.00 per child, per week.

Part-Time Weekly Fees

If your child will only attend 20 hours per week or less...
You pay $50.00 per child, per week.

Additional Fees

Drop-In Rates

Before-School Drop-In Rate (2 times per week at most):
$4.00 per child, per day.
After-School Drop-In Rate (Not counting as part-time):
$7.00 per child, per day.
Pre-School Drop-In Rate:
$15.00 per child, per day.
School Closing Drop-In Rate:
$12.00 per child, per day.

Miscellaneous Fees

Registration Fee:
$25.00 per family, non-refundable.
Processing Fee (For those not using Tuition Express to make payments):
$5.00 per month.
Bus Fee:
$10.00 per child, per semester.
School Closing Fee:
$5.00 per child, per day.
Early Dismissal Fee (For dismissals two hours early or greater):
$2.00 per child, per day.
