Our Pre-School Program

Daily Schedule

6:30AM
Center opens.
8:15AM
Breakfast is served.
8:45AM
Breakfast cleanup and bathroom break.
9:00AM
Music and movement.
9:15AM
Circle time – We will, as a group, come together and go over the calendar, the weather, the Pledge of Allegiance, and have a Bible lesson that will include Scripture, music, and an object lesson.
9:30AM
Organized recreation in the gym or on the playground. On Fridays, we have show and tell during this time.
9:45AM
Small groups – We will divide the class into several groups. Each group is structured based on the child’s development level, strengths, and areas where improvement is needed. We will focus on letters, numbers, colors, animals, art, and kindergarten readiness skills.
10:05AM
Recreation in the gym or on the playground.
10:30AM
Bibly study and craft.
11:15AM
Lunch is served.
11:50AM
Lunch cleanup, bathroom break, and preparation for rest time.
12:15PM
Rest time - Each child will have a mat to lie on and rest. Sleeping is encouraged but not required. (If you do not want your child to sleep, you may provide a soft toy for them to play with on their mat during this time.)
1:45PM
Recreation inside and/or outside.
2:30PM
Bathroom break, prepare for snack time.
2:45PM
Snack is served.
3:00PM
Free-reading time.
3:15PM
Arts and crafts.
4:00PM
Free-play time.
5:20PM
Clean up.
5:30PM
Music and movement.
6:00PM
Go home.
